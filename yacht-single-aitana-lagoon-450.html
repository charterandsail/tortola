Lagoon 450 SporTop 'Aitana'

A large, accessible and high-performance catamaran. A sail area worthy of a racing boat!

An intelligent deck layout: a comprehensive, safe steeringposition where it is easy for 2 people to operate, wide sidedecks, flush deck hatches and well-placed grabrails. A high-performance boat in all weathers.

A fabulous playground and platform for living aboard. With numerous spaces: forward cockpit, sunbathing area, aft cockpit… The aft cockpit also has a sunbathing area and direct access to the helming station.
